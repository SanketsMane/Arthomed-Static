Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Arthomed account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Use the Delete Account button in the Arthomed app (available in your user profile), or
  2. Send an email to: queries@arthomed.in
  3. Include your registered mobile number or email address used to log in.
  4. Specify that you want your account and all associated data deleted.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Appointment records
  • Medical documents and treatment records
  • Clinical notes
  • User preferences and settings
  • Chat history and communications

Data that may be retained:

  • Anonymized or aggregated analytics data used for app improvement
  • Required legal or regulatory records (if applicable)
  • Transaction records required for financial compliance

Retention & Processing Time:

Your request will be processed within 90 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

Contact Information:

  • Email: queries@arthomed.in
  • Phone: +91 99027 58444
  • Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM IST

Additional Information:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all data, appointments, and medical records
  • If you wish to use Arthomed again, you will need to create a new account
  • Active subscriptions will be cancelled upon account deletion
